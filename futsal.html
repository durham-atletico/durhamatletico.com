Register

Registration happens in two steps:

  • Fill out the registration form
  • Pay the registration fee on PayPal

We have budgeted this league for $90 a player. This will allow us to pay our expenses and purchase goals we need in the spring. However, we don't want anyone to be turned away for lack of funds. If this is an issue, please pay what you can afford (contact us at info@durhamatletico.com). We'd prefer that you pay via PayPal, but you can also pay with cash, check or credit card onsite at your first game.
