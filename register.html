Winter 2015 Futsal Registration

Registration happens in two steps:

1. Fill out the registration form

We need to collect some info about you. Fill out this form here.

2. Pay the fee online

Next, you need to pay the $90 registration fee via PayPal. You don't need a PayPal account to do so you can use your credit card.

You can also pay at your first game via cash or check.
